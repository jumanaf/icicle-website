Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

We allow you to move FAST using AI, with no lift from your end. Icicle will set up your future-proofed workflow, filtering out all the noise to select the best tools to work with your systems.

We also provide data science solutions, aloowing you to get insights from raw data to visualizations at speed.

How do you work with clients?

We start with a consultation to understand your needs, whether they be to create automations, complete an analysis of your complex data, or future-proof your systems with AI. We conduct an exploration and project outline stage, where we lay out exact project plans, and check in with you at your preferred cadence to provide a high-quality and high-value product.

Depending on your product, we provide maintanence, bug fixes, and updates as needed beyond the project.

How much do your services cost?
Pricing depends on the scope and complexity of your project. We offer: hourly consulting for small projects, fixed project pricing for well-defined tasks, and ongoing support retainers for long-term collaborations.
